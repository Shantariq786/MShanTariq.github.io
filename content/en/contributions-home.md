contributions:
  - badge: "Generalized KdV"
    title: "Improved Conservative DG Formulation"
    summary: "Developing a conservative DG method for the generalized KdV equation that preserves mass, energy, and Hamiltonian while removing the time-derivative term introduced by earlier numerical flux choices."
    impact: "The revised flux design lowers computational cost, supports higher-order implicit Runge-Kutta methods such as IRK4, and reduces each step to one nonlinear solve followed by one linear solve."

  - badge: "HS-KdV System"
    title: "Conservative DG Methods for Coupled Nonlinear Waves"
    summary: "Designing a new conservative DG framework for the coupled Hirota-Satsuma KdV system, with numerical traces chosen to eliminate derivative terms while preserving the main invariants."
    impact: "This extends structure-preserving DG ideas to a more complex coupled system and targets strong conservation, long-time stability, and optimal convergence behavior."

  - badge: "Research Path"
    title: "From KdV Analysis to BBM and NLS"
    summary: "My completed analytical work includes N-soliton solutions of the KdV equation and analytical solutions of the cylindrical KdV equation via the Bäcklund transformation."
    impact: "That foundation now supports my current DG research, with near-term plans for the BBM equation and future work on nonlinear Schrodinger-type models such as NLS."
